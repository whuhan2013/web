Here's a time line of President. Jiang Zemin's life:

  • 1926 - Born in the city of Yangzhou, Jiangsu
  • 1947 - Graduated from National Chiao Tung University in 1947 with a bachelor's degree in electrical engineering
  • 1949 - Jiang married Wang Yeping in 1949, also a native of Yangzhou.[4] She graduated from Shanghai International Studies University.[5] They have two sons, Jiang Mianheng and Jiang Miankang
  • 1950s - Received his training at the Stalin Automobile Works in Moscow,worked for Changchun's First Automobile Works
  • 1985 - Became Mayor of Shanghai, and subsequently the Party Secretary of Shanghai
  • 1989 - Jiang was elevated to the country's top job in 1989 with a fairly small power base inside the party
  • 1992 - Deng grew critical of Jiang's leadership in 1992. During Deng's southern tours, he subtly suggested that the pace of reform was not fast enough, and the "central leadership" (i.e. Jiang) had most responsibility
  • 1997 - Jiang delivered the elder statesman's eulogy and come to power eventually.
  • 2002 - Jiang stepped down from the powerful Politburo Standing Committee and as General Secretary to make way for a "fourth generation" of leadership headed by Hu Jintao
